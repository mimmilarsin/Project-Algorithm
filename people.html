Here you’ll get a peek at each team member and the cool stuff they’ve been up to. Everyone’s brought their own magic to the mix, tackling different pieces of the puzzle to make this project happen. Oh, and just a heads-up, the photos, not the real deal.

Mimmi Larsin, 29

Gothenburg

Given my background in web development and my studies in the Web Editor program at the University of Borås, I felt confident taking on the responsibility of developing the website for this project. I have created several websites in the past, which made it a natural choice for me to lead this part of the project. I created a project and repository on GitHub, where I designed and built the four pages of the website. Additionally, I crafted the CSS code and integrated images to ensure a visually appealing layout. My goal was to create an engaging platform that effectively showcases our project's data and findings.

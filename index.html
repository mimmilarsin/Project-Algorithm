The assignment

This assignment involves working in a group to collect and process a dataset, as well as developing an algorithm to analyze the data in a meaningful way. We have created a website that brings our dataset, algorithm, and project details together, showcasing both the data and the steps we’ve taken to work with it. Our team chose to work with data on research collaborations from the DiVA portal, focusing on publications from 2022 and 2023. The website provides insights into our project, from the dataset itself to how we’ve used our algorithm to visualize and analyze collaboration patterns.
